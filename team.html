WE ARE TEAM KART


Team KART is the official Formula Student team of IIT Kharagpur. Started in 2008 by a small group of students led by Mr. Siddhartha Khastgir, with spanners in their hands and passion in their hearts, the project intends to explore the practical side of automotive engineering.

Comprised of enthusiastic, hardworking and talented undergraduate students, our team prides itself on a constantly improving performance over the years.

We are people who put hours in greasy pants at our workshop, where sleepless nights become a common occurrence, all in pursuit of the goal of designing, building and racing a winning Formula Student car.
